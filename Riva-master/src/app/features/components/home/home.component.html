Common Questions

How do I track my blood sugar?

You can use our dashboard to log your readings manually or sync with supported smart devices for real-time updates.

Is my medical data secure?

Yes, we use end-to-end encryption to ensure your health records are only visible to you and your doctor.

What are the early symptoms of Alzheimer's?

Early symptoms include memory loss, difficulty finding words, confusion about time or place, and problems with daily tasks.

How can I reduce my risk of heart disease

You can reduce your risk by eating a balanced diet, exercising regularly, avoiding smoking, managing stress, and checking your blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
